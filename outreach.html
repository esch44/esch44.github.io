I am currently Vice President of and peer mentor for the student-founded, student led Spectrum group at George Mason University. The group focuses on diversity, equity, and inclusion for underrepresented groups in STEM. Spectrum hosts professional development lunch talks that aim to level the playing ground for all students of all career stages. The mentoring program begun by Spectrum is the first official mentoring program within the Department of Physics and Astronomy. Spectrum matches each mentee with two mentors in order to provide support to underrepresented students within the department.
